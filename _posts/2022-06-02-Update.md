### IFTTT

So, I've been using this cool little platform called <a href='https://ifttt.com/'>IFTTT</a> (IF This Then That). It's pretty self-explanatory, but essentially what you do is setup an 'applet' to receive data from one popular service, and post that data in whatever format you please to another service, without writing your own program and obtaining API keys.
